Gothenburg & the West Coast

Tour Highlights

  • Explore the cultural highlights of Gothenburg
  • Take an evening food tour
  • Drive up the spectacular west coast
  • Kayak around the Koster Islands
  • Enjoy a cycling tour of the countryside

Key Facts

  • Days: 5
  • Accommodation: Medium

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Gothenburg and transfer to your hotel for your first night. Meet your guide and enjoy an afternoon tour of the key cultural sights.

Day 2: Free day to explore the city at your own leisure, then in the evening take a private food tour with your local guide.

Day 3: Head into the countryside today and have a full day's cycling tour with your expert guide. Enjoy a picnic in the forest.

Day 4: Drive up the west coast to Strömstad and then hop over to the Koster Islands on the ferry. Spend the afternoon relaxing on the beach and exploring by kayak.

Day 5: Return to Gothenburg by ferry and car for your onward journey.
