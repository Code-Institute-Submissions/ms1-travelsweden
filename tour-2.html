  • See the Northern Lights

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Kiruna and transfer to your hotel for your first night in the Arctic. Meet your guide and enjoy an afternoon hike of the surrounding scenery.

Day 2: First full day in Kiruna. Spend the morning relaxing in the hotel spa at your leisure before enjoying an afternoon dog sledding experience. In the evening, see the Northern Lights.

Day 3: Take a full day hike through the mountains with your private guide including a lunchtime BBQ.

Day 4: Transfer east by train and stay overnight in a unique treetop hotel.

Day 5: Head to Luleå and explore the city before journeying home.
