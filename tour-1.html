Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Stockholm and transfer to your hotel for your first night in the capital. Meet your guide and enjoy an afternoon walking tour of Gamla Stan, the city's old town.

Day 2: First full day in Stockholm. Spend the morning exploring at your leisure before enjoying a boat trip to Drottningholm, a beautiful palace set in the countryside.

Day 3: Take the ferry into the archielago and spend the remainder of the day enjoying the views over the water from your hotel.

Day 4: Enjoy a guided forest walk with your guide, including a traditional Swedish picnic lunch in a secluded area.

Day 5: Return to Stockholm by ferry and enjoy a final farewell dinner in a restaurant overlooking the waters.
